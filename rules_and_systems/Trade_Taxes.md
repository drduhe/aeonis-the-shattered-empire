# Aeonis: Trade

> **Chapter hygiene (Plan 6, 2026-07-13):** This file replaces the stale “Trade and Taxes” chapter. Buildings pay their full cost up front and have no recurring resource upkeep (`Buildings.md`). Advanced-unit upkeep lives in `Advanced_Units.md`; Council tax laws live in `High_Council.md`.

---

## 1. Trade action

On your turn in the **Action Phase**, you may spend **1 AP** to initiate a **Trade** with one other player (once per round).

Negotiate openly. A completed Trade may exchange any combination of:

| Tradeable | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Gold, Mana, Influence** | Any amounts agreed |
| **Remnants** | Any number |
| **Utility Artifacts** | Freely tradeable (see `Artifacts.md`) |
| **Hex control** | Transfer control of a hex you control (subject to units/buildings already there) |
| **Unit gifts** | Gift units you own to the other player (place legally) |
| **Promises** | Future support, non-aggression, council votes — enforced only as the table enforces deals (`Diplomacy.md`) |

**Not tradeable:**

- **Population** (cap/pool — not a currency)
- **VP, Renown, Whisper Cards in hand, objectives**
- **Lord Equipment** and **Building Relics** (transfer only via capture / hex control change — `Artifacts.md`)
- **Legendary Buildings** (control follows the City hex)

---

## 2. Portals

Trades between players who both control or occupy hexes connected by **Portal** travel may be initiated at **0 AP** (still once per round). See `Movement.md` / `Tiles.md`.

---

## 3. Markets (not Trade Hubs)

There is no separate “Trade Hub” region system. The **Market** building (`Buildings.md`) and Lord abilities (e.g. Cassian) own trade-economy bonuses.

---

## 4. Restrictions

- **Once per round** initiate (0 AP Portal trades still count as your one initiate).
- Distance limits are **optional** table variants; default is open negotiation.

---

## 5. Strategic notes

Trade is deliberate (AP-gated), supports diplomacy without formal contracts by default, and keeps Population / VP / Whispers out of the market so those systems stay crisp.
