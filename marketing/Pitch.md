## One-liner

**An empire has fallen. Eight banished Lords ride to claim its throne.** Aeonis is an epic fantasy strategy game of armies, councils, and forbidden magic for 3-8 players — in the tradition of the great galactic epics, reforged in steel and sorcery.

## Elevator pitch (~100 words)

A century ago the Eternal Empire shattered, and magic vanished with it. Now the Speaking Stones whisper again, and the banished Lords return — each commanding a faction with its own armies, powers, and claim to the Imperial Seat. Aeonis is a 3-8 player epic strategy game of territorial conquest, high politics, and rediscovered magic. March your Lord at the head of your army (and risk their capture), scheme with one-shot Whisper cards, vote in a High Council that writes the realm's laws mid-game, and race to 10 victory points across an evening you'll be retelling for weeks.

## The hooks (campaign page section headers)

1. **Your Lord rides with the army.** Every faction's leader is a piece on the map — a hero in battle and a hostage if you overreach. Capturing an enemy Lord scores points and shuts down their powers.
2. **The council writes the rules.** Every round, the High Council convenes. Propose laws, buy votes with Influence, redraw borders, or tax your rivals — the realm's rules are on the table.
3. **The Stones are listening.** Whisper cards are intelligence made playable: ambushes, vetoes, sabotage, and secrets deployed at exactly the wrong moment for your enemies.
4. **Magic is a tech tree with teeth.** Five schools of Arcane Discovery — war magic, wards, divination, transmutation, and geomancy — plus a forbidden sixth for those who dare.
5. **A war of land, not lanes.** Terrain, zones of control, cavalry flanking, and sieges make every border a decision.
6. **Relics of a fallen empire.** Explore ruins, gather Remnants, and claim 24 unique artifacts — a few of which are worth the throne itself.

## For fans of…

Twilight Imperium, Eclipse, Heroes of Land Air & Sea, Oath — if your group lives for the big table, the long game, and the story after, Aeonis was designed for you. Sessions run 4-10 hours depending on player count; the short-game variant trims an evening game for weeknights.

## Boilerplate (press/BGG description)

*Aeonis: The Shattered Empire* is an epic fantasy strategy board game for 3-8 players. Lead one of eight asymmetric Lords in a contest for the Imperial Seat: conquer hex territories, legislate in the High Council, research Arcane Discoveries, unearth artifacts of the fallen empire, and deploy Whisper cards at the perfect moment. First to 10 victory points claims the throne. Designed for the heavy-strategy table in the tradition of the genre's greatest epics.
