## 4. Funding goal calculation

```
Funding goal = Fixed costs
             + (Landed cost × MOQ 1,500)
             + fees on goal (~11%)
             + 15% contingency
```

- Public goal = the **minimum viable print run** number, not the hoped raise (see `Comps_Analysis.md` §3).
- Sanity check: goal ÷ average pledge = backers needed. Compare with email-list size × 3-5% conversion (industry heuristic: list conversion 3-10%; day-one from list ≈ 30-50% of final).
