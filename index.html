What Aeonis is trying to do (on purpose)

The goal is a grand strategy experience that stays readable at the table: clear windows, meaningful constraints, and factions that change how you negotiate and expand.

1

Clear timing spine

A canonical round structure with explicit windows, so “when can I do X?” has one answer.

Round Structure
2

Meaningful constraints

Population as a growth limiter; upkeep pressures only when it matters; borders that make positioning real.

Population • Movement
3

Asymmetry with a setting spine

Speaking Stones, rediscovered magic, banished lords — lore that drives objectives, politics, and faction identity.
