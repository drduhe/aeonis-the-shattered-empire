TI4-scale fantasy grand strategy

Claim the Shattered Throne — through war, wizardry, wealth, or will.

In a realm ruled by the whispers of the Speaking Stones, Lords rebuild empires from ruin. Aeonis: The Shattered Empire is a “big strategy” board game of rotating turns, ruthless diplomacy, arcane discovery, and border wars across a living map.

Best for tables that love negotiation, timing windows, and “one more turn” deals.

A bold strategy sandbox

Aeonis combines the sweep of 4X with sharp tactical constraints: population caps, contested borders, and one action per turn.

Rotating actions

Spend Action Points for movement, building, diplomacy, trade, and arcane progress — while opponents respond between turns.

Politics with teeth

The High Council reshapes the board: laws, decrees, titles, and timing windows for negotiation and betrayal.
