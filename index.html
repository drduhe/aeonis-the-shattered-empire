Aeonis: The Shattered Empire

The throne has been empty for a hundred years, and it kills its claimants. Eight banished Lords return to take it anyway — through armies, laws, trade, and resurgent magic. In the tradition of the great epic 4X board games.

3–8 players
4–10 hrs one epic session
