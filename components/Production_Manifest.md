# Aeonis: Production Component Manifest (Full Game)

**Purpose:** the manufacturable bill of materials for quoting (Phase 5 of the roadmap). This is the **full retail game** — the First Playable prototype list stays in `Components.md`. Baseline per locked decision D3 (`../marketing/Positioning.md`): **acrylic standees** for all units and Lords; **custom Lord miniatures** are a stretch-goal SKU quoted separately.

Counts assume **8 players max**, 8 launch factions. Quantities marked ~ need playtest confirmation.
