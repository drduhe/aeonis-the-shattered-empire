# Lord Sheet: Merchant Prince

Theme: trades, Influence leverage, and economic tempo.

---

## Starting Setup

- **Starting AP**: 5
- **Starting resources**: 3 Gold, 2 Mana, 2 Influence
- **Population Cap / Pool**: 10 / 10
- **Starting units**: 2 Infantry, 1 Archer (in Home City)

### Unique Starting Tile (replaces your Plains)

**Caravan Bazaar** (counts as **Desert**)

- **Production**: +2 Influence
- **Building permissions**: You may build an **Embassy** here, but you may **not** build a **Tower** here.
- **Controller benefit**: When a player initiates a Trade at 0 AP (e.g., via Market), the controller of Caravan Bazaar gains **+1 Gold** (max once per round).

---

## Faction Ability: Favor Broker

Once per round, when you **initiate a Trade**, reduce its AP cost by **1** (minimum 0).

---

## Faction Ability: Council Patronage

Once per round, after a High Council vote resolves, if you spent **any** Influence on lobbying for that vote, gain **+1 Gold**.

---

## First Playable Lord Objective (2 VP)

**Coins and Crowns**: End a round with **10 Gold** OR win a **Title** in the High Council.
