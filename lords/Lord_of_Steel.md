# Lord Sheet: Lord of Steel

Theme: durable fronts, strongholds, and battlefield control.

---

## Starting Setup

- **Starting AP**: 5
- **Starting resources**: 4 Gold, 1 Mana, 1 Influence
- **Population Cap / Pool**: 10 / 10
- **Starting units**: 3 Infantry (in Home City)

### Unique Starting Tile (replaces your Mountain)

**Ironworks Ridge** (counts as **Mountain**)

- **Production**: +2 Gold
- **Building permissions**: You may build a **Fortress** here. If you build a **Mine** here, reduce its Gold build cost by **1**.
- **Owner-only benefit** (only while you control it): Once per round, when you build a **Fortress**, reduce the build cost by **1 Gold**.

---

## Faction Ability: Forged in Battle

Your units gain **+1 Defense** when defending a hex with one of your **buildings**.

---

## Faction Ability: Siegecraft

When you spend an **Attack** action against a **Siege**, you may resolve **Press the Attack** by paying **0 AP** (still limited to once per Attack).

---

## First Playable Lord Objective (2 VP)

**Unbroken Line**: Control a **Fortress** hex and win a battle where you are the defender.
