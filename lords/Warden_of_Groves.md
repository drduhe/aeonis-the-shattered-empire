# Lord Sheet: Warden of Groves

Theme: population growth, holding ground, and map shaping.

---

## Starting Setup

- **Starting AP**: 5
- **Starting resources**: 2 Gold, 3 Mana, 1 Influence
- **Population Cap / Pool**: 11 / 11
- **Starting units**: 2 Infantry, 1 Archer (in Home City)

### Unique Starting Tile (replaces your Forest)

**Sacred Grove** (counts as **Forest**)

- **Production**: +1 Mana and +1 Population
- **Building permissions**: You may build a **Tower** here, but you may **not** build a **Grove** here.
- **Owner-only benefit** (only while you control it): At the end of Production & Upkeep, gain **+1 Population Pool** (up to cap).

---

## Faction Ability: Verdant Growth

At the end of **Production & Upkeep**, gain **+1 Population Pool** (up to cap).

---

## Faction Ability: Rooted Defenses

Once per battle round, when defending in a **Forest/Grove** hex, you may reroll **one** defense die.

---

## First Playable Lord Objective (2 VP)

**Green Dominion**: Control **3 Forest/Grove** hexes at Cleanup & Checks.
