2 · What chaos proved — and what it didn't

Chaos data IS valid for…

  • Engine stability under stress
  • Rules encoding completeness (ledger entries)
  • Map scaling across player counts
  • Structural incentive shape when nobody tries
  • Verdict/triage signatures for degenerate play

Chaos data is NOT valid for…

  • Lord win rates or matchup balance
  • Seat-position fairness
  • Catch-up / runaway-leader conclusions
  • Combat reward tuning
  • Any canon change justified by VP percentages
Do not tune balance from chaos reports. The unified 600-game playtest report (2026-07-02-playtest-report-all-player-counts.html) is shareable as an engineering milestone, not a design verdict. Plan B persona tournaments are the first source of balance statistics per the design spec.

6 · Alignment with improvement plans

The chaos baseline creates tension with proposed design changes already on the roadmap:

  • Plan 3 (VP legibility) targets ≥60% of winner VP from objectives. Chaos shows 63% from Seat + streak combined — the opposite distribution. Plan B must determine whether strategic play moves toward Plan 3's target or confirms Seat dominance is structural.
  • Plan 1 (combat aggression) assumes rational players don't attack. Chaos confirms combat VP is ~2% — but doesn't test whether Warmonger personas can make combat a viable path. H5 gates this.
  • Plan 4 (high player count) flags 6p/8p validation as a group-play gate. Engine scaling passes; pacing at 7p needs persona validation (H4).

8 · Known fast-follows (non-blocking)

From Milestone 1 code review. Safe to fix during Plan B implementation:

  • Replay verdict mismatch: replay() returns completed for games recorded as degenerate — VP monitor lives in runner, not engine.
  • Recorded rounds off by one: record["rounds"] increments before game end; balance stats should use played rounds = recorded − 1.
  • No crash-record capture: runner doesn't save partial records on invariant violation (none observed in 600 games).
  • Unit test gaps: apply_defender_retreat, press AP flow, guard verdict paths untested directly.
