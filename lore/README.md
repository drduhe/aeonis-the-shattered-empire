# Lore folder

Canonical narrative material for **Aeonis: The Shattered Empire**. Rules live in `rules_and_systems/`; this folder is flavor and naming canon.

## Contents

- **`Lore.md`** — The canonical lorebook: the timeline of the Eternal Empire and the Shattering, the Speaking Stones, the Imperial Seat, the geography of Aeonis (all major regions and each faction's homeland), the eight Banished Lords and their races, magic and the arcane schools, artifacts and Remnants, the faiths of Aeonis, legends and mysteries, and seeds for future factions.
- **`Naming_Bible.md`** — The naming and terminology canon: the defined-terms glossary (canonical terms and forbidden synonyms), the faction/race roster, region names, per-culture naming conventions with example names for future content, and a pronunciation guide.

## How this folder relates to the rest of the repo

- `rules_and_systems/Lore.md` is the short **rules-side setting foundation**; this folder's `Lore.md` is the full expansion of it. The two must never contradict each other.
- The eight Lord sheets in `lords/` are the **primary source** for each Lord's personal lore, race, and faction name. The lorebook harvests and expands them; if a Lord sheet changes, update the corresponding subsection here.
- When writing any new content (cards, events, Lord sheets, flavor text), check `Naming_Bible.md` first — it defines the canonical terms (e.g., "Imperial Seat", "Speaking Stones", "Influence") and the forbidden synonyms.

## Editing rules

- Never contradict a fact established on a Lord sheet or in `rules_and_systems/Lore.md`.
- No game-rules text in `Lore.md` — flavor only. The Naming Bible's terminology table is the one rules-adjacent exception.
- New proper nouns (people, places, factions) must be added to `Naming_Bible.md` when introduced.
